I am a Master's student in Cyberspace Security at [Xidian University](https://www.xidian.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. Hui Zhu. My research interests lie in **applied cryptography**, **privacy-preserving computation**, and **homomorphic encryption**, with a focus on building secure and efficient systems using Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) and searchable encryption techniques.
